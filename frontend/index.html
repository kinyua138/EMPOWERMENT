About Us

Empowerment Fund Kenya is powered by World Bank grant programs, focused on rapid and equitable distribution.

This platform is a simple, dignified engine — a clear path from hope to funding. It is designed to be accessible, fast, and trustworthy, giving eligible Kenyans the confidence to apply and dare to build.

Empowering Community

Empowerment Fund Kenya aims to promote economic growth and improve the living standards of Kenyan people. The initiative responds to challenges such as drought, famine, and economic instability by rolling out structured grant programs that strengthen local enterprises.

Grants are available to all Kenyans above 18 years, with an annual allocation supporting startups and existing businesses facing financial challenges.

Who Can Apply?

Empowerment Fund Kenya grants are available to all eligible Kenyans across every county. Here's what you need to know:

Basic Requirements

  • Must be a Kenyan citizen aged 18 years and above
  • Valid national ID or passport
  • Proof of residence in Kenya
  • Active bank account (for fund disbursement)

Business Requirements

  • Clear business plan or concept
  • Demonstrated need for funding
  • Commitment to business development
  • Willingness to participate in training programs

Grant-Specific Eligibility

Business Start-up Grants

  • New business ventures
  • Innovative ideas with market potential
  • Individual entrepreneurs or groups

Business Boost Grants

  • Existing businesses facing challenges
  • Businesses affected by economic conditions
  • Enterprises needing expansion capital

Youth Innovation Fund

  • Ages 18-35 years
  • Technology and innovation focus
  • Youth-led initiatives

What Disqualifies an Application?

  • Non-Kenyan citizens
  • Individuals under 18 years
  • Incomplete or fraudulent applications
  • Businesses involved in illegal activities
  • Previous grant recipients who haven't met reporting requirements

Application Process

  1. Online Application: Submit your application through our secure portal
  2. Document Verification: Provide required identification and business documents
  3. Review Process: Applications are reviewed within 48 hours
  4. Approval & Funding: Successful applicants receive funds directly to their bank accounts

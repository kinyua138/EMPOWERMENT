General Questions

Empowerment Fund Kenya is a World Bank-backed initiative designed to provide grants to Kenyan entrepreneurs and businesses. Our mission is to promote economic growth and improve living standards by supporting startups and existing businesses across all 47 counties.

Any Kenyan citizen aged 18 years and above can apply. We support individuals, groups, and businesses across all sectors and counties. Specific eligibility criteria may vary by grant type.

Funding amounts vary by grant type:

  • Business Start-up: Up to KES 500,000
  • Business Boost: Up to KES 1,000,000
  • Youth Innovation Fund: Up to KES 750,000

Yes, applying for grants through Empowerment Fund Kenya is completely free. There are no application fees, processing fees, or hidden costs.

Application Process

The application process is simple and online:

  1. Check your eligibility on our website
  2. Complete the online application form
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Wait for review (typically within 48 hours)
  5. Receive approval and funding if successful

Required documents typically include:

  • National ID or passport
  • Proof of residence
  • Bank account details
  • Business plan or concept description
  • Additional documents may be required based on grant type

We strive to review all applications within 48 hours. Successful applicants receive their funding directly to their bank accounts within 7-10 business days after approval.

Yes, you can apply for different grant types if you meet the eligibility criteria. However, you can only receive one grant at a time. Previous grant recipients must complete their reporting requirements before applying for additional funding.

Grant Management

Grant funds can be used for legitimate business purposes including:

  • Purchasing equipment and supplies
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Working capital
  • Training and capacity building
  • Business expansion activities

Funds cannot be used for personal expenses, debt repayment, or illegal activities.

No, our grants are non-repayable. They are designed to support business growth and do not need to be returned. However, recipients are required to submit progress reports and may participate in training programs.

Grant recipients are required to:

  • Submit quarterly progress reports
  • Participate in mandatory training sessions
  • Maintain proper financial records
  • Report on business performance metrics

These requirements help us ensure funds are used effectively and provide support for your business success.
